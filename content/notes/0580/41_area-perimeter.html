Perimeter is the distance around. Area is the space inside. Six shapes cover every non-circle question.

Area formulas
RectangleA = l × w
TriangleA = ½bh
ParallelogramA = bh
TrapeziumA = ½(a + b)h
Rhombus / kiteA = ½ × d₁ × d₂
Triangle by two sides + angle (Extended)
A = ½ · a · b · sin C
Example Trapezium with parallel sides 7 cm and 11 cm, height 6 cm. A = ½(7+11)(6) = 54 cm².
h is perpendicular The h in a triangle/parallelogram area is the perpendicular height from base — not the slant side. Drop a perpendicular if needed.
