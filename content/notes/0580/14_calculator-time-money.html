Utility topics examiners still test: calculator use, 24-hour clock, currency.

Calculator
  • Bracket everything you'd group mentally — e.g. (4+3)/(2−5), not 4+3/2−5
  • Use Ans and the fraction key; avoid copying decimals back in
  • For exact surds/trig, don't round until the final step
Time
1 min = 60 s · 1 h = 60 min · 1 day = 24 h · 1 year = 365 days (366 in leap year)
24-hour clock 10:45 → 14:15 is 3 h 30 min = 3.5 h = 210 min. Be careful crossing midnight.
Money & currency
Convertamount × exchange rate (check direction!)
Example £250 at 1.15 €/£ → 250 × 1.15 = €287.50. Coming back: €287.50 ÷ 1.15 = £250.
Decimal pennies Always give money answers to 2 dp. 3.7 → 3.70 — write the trailing zero.
