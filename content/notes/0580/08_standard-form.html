Standard form packs very big / very small numbers into one tidy expression.

FormA × 10ⁿ where 1 ≤ A < 10, n ∈ ℤ
Converting
Big number → std formcount digits moved LEFT to n
Small number → std formcount digits moved RIGHT to −n
Example 6 300 000 = 6.3 × 10⁶ · 0.00042 = 4.2 × 10⁻⁴
Arithmetic (3 × 10⁵) × (2 × 10³) = 6 × 10⁸ · (8 × 10⁶) ÷ (4 × 10²) = 2 × 10⁴
Adjusting A Calculator may give 32 × 10⁴ — not standard. Rewrite as 3.2 × 10⁵. The constraint 1 ≤ A < 10 is non-negotiable.
