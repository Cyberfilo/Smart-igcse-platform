Round every input to 1 significant figure, do the calculation, state "estimate ≈ …". Quick sanity check on calculator answers.

Rounding rules
Decimal places (dp)count from the point
Significant figures (sf)count from first non-zero
Example 0.04087 to 2 sf = 0.041 (leading zeros don't count as sig. figs.)
Estimating (19.8 × 4.92) / 2.05 ≈ (20 × 5) / 2 = 50. Actual: 47.5 — estimate is close enough to spot a calculator slip.
Estimating √ √83 lies between √81 (= 9) and √100 (= 10), closer to 9 → about 9.1. Actual: 9.11.
