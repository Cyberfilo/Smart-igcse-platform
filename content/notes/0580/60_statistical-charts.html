Match chart type to data type. Read from the axes; comment on comparisons.

Chart types
  • Bar chart: discrete/qualitative · heights give frequency
  • Pictogram: symbol key tells you how many each icon represents
  • Pie chart: 360° / total = angle per unit
  • Frequency polygon: midpoints connected by straight lines
  • Dual / comparative bar: side-by-side for two groups
  • Stem-and-leaf: preserves every raw value, shape at a glance
Pie-chart angles
angle for category = (category count / total) × 360°
Example Pie chart of 90 students: 30 like football. Angle = (30/90)·360° = 120°.
Reading pictograms Always check the key. "½ symbol = 5 people" is common — don't just count icons.
