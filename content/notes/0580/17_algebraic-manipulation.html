Collect like terms, expand brackets, factorise. These three moves unlock every equation question.

Expanding brackets
Single\(a(b + c) = ab + ac\)
Double (FOIL)\((a+b)(c+d) = ac + ad + bc + bd\)
Perfect square\((a+b)^{2} = a^{2} + 2ab + b^{2}\)
Diff. of squares\((a+b)(a-b) = a^{2} - b^{2}\)
Factorising
  • Common factor: \(6x^{2} + 9x = 3x(2x + 3)\)
  • Difference of squares: \(x^{2} - 16 = (x-4)(x+4)\)
  • Quadratic \(x^{2} + bx + c\): find two numbers that multiply to \(c\), add to \(b\)
  • Extended — \(ax^{2} + bx + c\): split middle term, then factor by grouping
Example Factorise \(2x^{2} + 7x + 3\). Split: \(2x^{2} + 6x + x + 3 = 2x(x+3) + 1(x+3) = (2x+1)(x+3)\).
