Collect like terms, expand brackets, factorise. These three moves unlock every equation question.

Expanding brackets
Singlea(b + c) = ab + ac
Double (FOIL)(a+b)(c+d) = ac+ad+bc+bd
Perfect square(a+b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
Diff. of squares(a+b)(a−b) = a² − b²
Factorising
  • Common factor: 6x² + 9x = 3x(2x + 3)
  • Difference of squares: x² − 16 = (x−4)(x+4)
  • Quadratic x² + bx + c: find two numbers that multiply to c, add to b
  • Extended — ax² + bx + c: split middle term, then factor by grouping
Example Factorise 2x² + 7x + 3. Split: 2x² + 6x + x + 3 = 2x(x+3) + 1(x+3) = (2x+1)(x+3).
