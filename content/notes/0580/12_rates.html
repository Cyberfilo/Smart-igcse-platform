Rate = amount per unit. Same formula pattern for speed, density, pressure.

Common rates
Speedspeed = distance / time
Densityρ = mass / volume
PressureP = force / area
Flowrate = quantity / time
Average vs instantaneous
Average speed = total distance ÷ total time (NOT average of individual speeds)
Example Travel 30 km at 60 km/h, then 30 km at 30 km/h. Total time = 0.5 + 1 = 1.5 h. Average speed = 60/1.5 = 40 km/h.
Unit conversion km/h → m/s: multiply by 1000/3600 = 5/18. m/s → km/h: multiply by 18/5. Always check units match before combining rates.
