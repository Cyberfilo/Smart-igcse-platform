BIDMAS orders every calculation. Brackets first, last thing always: addition & subtraction.

OrderBrackets → Indices → Division/Multiplication (left→right) → Addition/Subtraction (left→right)
Signed numbers
Adding\(+\ +\ - = -\)  |  \(-\ -\ - = +\)
Multiplying\((-)(-) = (+)\) · \((-)(+) = (-)\)
Example \(3 + 2 \times (7 - 4)^{2} = 3 + 2 \times 9 = 3 + 18 = 21\).
With negatives \(-6 + (-4) \times 3 = -6 + (-12) = -18\). \((-2)^{3} = -8\). \((-2)^{4} = 16\).
Watch out \(-3^{2} = -9\) (negation applied last). But \((-3)^{2} = 9\). Brackets change the meaning.
