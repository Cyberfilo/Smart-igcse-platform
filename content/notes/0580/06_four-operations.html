BIDMAS orders every calculation. Brackets first, last thing always: addition & subtraction.

OrderBrackets → Indices → Division/Multiplication (left→right) → Addition/Subtraction (left→right)
Signed numbers
Adding+ + − = −  |  − − − = +
Multiplying(−)(−) = (+) · (−)(+) = (−)
Example 3 + 2 × (7 − 4)² = 3 + 2 × 9 = 3 + 18 = 21.
With negatives −6 + (−4) × 3 = −6 + (−12) = −18. (−2)³ = −8. (−2)⁴ = 16.
Watch out −3² = −9 (negation applied last). But (−3)² = 9. Brackets change the meaning.
