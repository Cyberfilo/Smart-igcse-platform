Four transformations move shapes without distorting angles. Specify each with exact parameters — no missing info.

Four transformations + parameters
  • Reflection: state the mirror line (e.g. "\(y = x\)", "\(x = 2\)")
  • Rotation: state centre, angle, direction (e.g. "\(90°\) clockwise about \((0, 0)\)")
  • Translation: state the column vector \(\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}\)
  • Enlargement: state scale factor AND centre (e.g. "SF \(2\), centre \((0, 0)\)")
Properties
Congruentreflection, rotation, translation
Not congruentenlargement (unless SF \(= 1\) or \(-1\))
Example (enlargement) Triangle \(A\) at \((1,1), (3,1), (1,2)\). Enlargement SF \(2\) about origin: \(A'\) at \((2,2), (6,2), (2,4)\). Sides double, area \(\times 4\).
Negative scale factor SF \(< 0\) flips the image through the centre of enlargement. SF \(= -1\) is rotation \(180°\) about that centre.
