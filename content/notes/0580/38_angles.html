Six reusable facts cover almost every angle question.

Core facts
  • Angles on a straight line add to 180°
  • Angles round a point add to 360°
  • Vertically opposite angles are equal
  • Triangle angles add to 180°
  • Exterior angle of a triangle = sum of two opposite interior angles
  • Quadrilateral angles add to 360°
Parallel lines
Corresponding (F)equal
Alternate (Z)equal
Co-interior (C)add to 180°
Polygons
Interior sum = (n − 2) × 180° · Exterior sum = 360° (any polygon)
Example Regular octagon interior angle: (8 − 2) × 180 / 8 = 135°. Exterior angle: 180 − 135 = 45°.
