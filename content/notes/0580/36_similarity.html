Similar triangle tests
  • AAA: all three angles match (only two needed — third is forced)
  • SSS: all three sides in the same ratio
  • SAS: two sides in the same ratio, included angle equal
Area example Two similar triangles have sides in ratio 2 : 5. Small area = 12 cm². Large area = 12 × (5/2)² = 75 cm².
Volume example Similar solids with surface area ratio 9 : 16. Length ratio = 3 : 4 (square-root). Volume ratio = 27 : 64.
Congruence Congruent = similar + same size (k = 1). Tests: SSS, SAS, ASA, RHS (right-angle, hypotenuse, side).
