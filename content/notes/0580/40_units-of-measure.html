Convert between metric units by sliding the decimal point. Unit errors lose the whole mark.

Metric conversions
Length1 km = 1000 m · 1 m = 100 cm · 1 cm = 10 mm
Area1 m² = 10 000 cm² · 1 cm² = 100 mm²
Volume1 m³ = 10⁶ cm³ · 1 cm³ = 1 ml
Capacity1 l = 1000 ml · 1 m³ = 1000 l
Mass1 t = 1000 kg · 1 kg = 1000 g
Time1 h = 3600 s · 1 day = 86 400 s
Example Convert 2.5 m³ to cm³: 2.5 × 10⁶ = 2 500 000 cm³.
Squared / cubed Area factor = (length factor)². Volume factor = (length factor)³. Not the same factor — 1 m = 100 cm, BUT 1 m² = 10 000 cm².
