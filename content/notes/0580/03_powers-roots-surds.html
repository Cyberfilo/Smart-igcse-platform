Powers are repeated multiplication; roots undo them. Surds keep exact irrational values.

Core powers & roots
Squarea² = a × a
Square root√a² = a (a ≥ 0)
Cubea³ = a × a × a
Cube root∛a³ = a
Surd rules (Extended)
√a × √b = √(ab)  |  √a ÷ √b = √(a/b)  |  (a + √b)(a − √b) = a² − b
Simplify √72 = √(36 × 2) = 6√2 · √18 + √50 = 3√2 + 5√2 = 8√2
Rationalise 3/√5 = 3√5/5 · 1/(2 + √3) = (2 − √3)/(4 − 3) = 2 − √3
Watch out √16 = 4 is rational. Only √n where n isn't a perfect square is irrational.
