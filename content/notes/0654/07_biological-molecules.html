Three food groups + their building blocks + their food tests. Memorise the triangle.

CarbohydratesC, H, O — made of glucose (sugars) or long chains (starch, cellulose)
ProteinsC, H, O, N — made of amino acids
Lipids (fats)C, H, O — made of 1 glycerol + 3 fatty acids
Food tests
  • Starch → iodine solution → blue-black
  • Reducing sugar → Benedict's + heat → brick-red precipitate (was blue)
  • Protein → Biuret → purple/violet (was blue)
  • Lipid → ethanol + water → white emulsion
  • Vitamin C → DCPIP (blue dye) → decolourised
Worked example Apple tested with Benedict's → brick-red; with iodine → stays brown. Contains reducing sugar, no starch.
Heat Benedict's Benedict's only reacts with heat. No heat = no colour change = looks like a negative result.
