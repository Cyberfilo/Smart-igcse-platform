A balanced diet has seven groups in the right proportions. Deficiency of any one causes a specific disorder.

Seven nutrients
  • Carbohydrate — main energy source (bread, rice)
  • Protein — growth and repair (meat, beans)
  • Lipid — long-term energy + insulation (butter, oil)
  • Vitamins — small amounts, specific roles (C, D)
  • Minerals — ions (Fe, Ca)
  • Water — solvent for reactions
  • Dietary fibre — gut movement
Deficiencies
Vit Cscurvy — bleeding gums
Vit D / Carickets — weak bones
Feanaemia — tired, pale
Proteinkwashiorkor — bloated abdomen
Needs change Pregnant women need more Ca + Fe. Growing children need more protein. Active adults need more energy.
