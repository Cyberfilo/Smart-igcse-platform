All living things share seven processes. The acronym MRS GREN covers them all.

MMovement
RRespiration
SSensitivity
GGrowth
RReproduction
EExcretion
NNutrition
Each, briefly
  • Movement — action by an organism (not passive drift)
  • Respiration — releasing energy from food chemically
  • Sensitivity — detecting and responding to stimuli
  • Growth — permanent increase in size and dry mass
  • Reproduction — making new individuals
  • Excretion — removing metabolic waste (not faeces — that's egestion)
  • Nutrition — taking in raw materials for energy and growth
Excretion ≠ egestion Faeces come from undigested food and are egested, not excreted. Excretion means metabolic waste like CO₂ and urea.
