An atom has a tiny dense nucleus (protons + neutrons) surrounded by electrons in shells.

Proton+1 charge, mass 1, in nucleus
Neutron0 charge, mass 1, in nucleus
Electron−1 charge, ~0 mass, in shells
Atomic notation
Top: Amass number = protons + neutrons
Bottom: Zatomic number = protons
Number of neutrons = A − Z
Electron shells
  • Shell 1: holds 2 electrons
  • Shell 2: holds 8
  • Shell 3: holds 8 (for IGCSE level)
  • Neutral atom: electrons = protons
Example — Na Na (Z=11, A=23): 11 protons, 12 neutrons, 11 electrons. Configuration: 2, 8, 1.
