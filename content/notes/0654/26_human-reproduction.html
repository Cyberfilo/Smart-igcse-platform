Male and female systems produce gametes. Fertilisation in the oviduct creates a zygote that implants in the uterus lining.

Male system
  • Testes — produce sperm + testosterone
  • Sperm duct → penis
  • Prostate gland → seminal fluid
Female system
  • Ovaries — produce eggs + oestrogen
  • Oviduct — site of fertilisation
  • Uterus — develops lining monthly
  • Vagina — birth canal
Menstrual cycle
  • Day 1–5: period (uterus lining sheds)
  • Day 5–14: lining rebuilds (oestrogen)
  • Day 14: ovulation (egg released)
  • Day 14–28: lining maintained (progesterone); sheds if no fertilisation
STIs
  • HIV — virus; attacks lymphocytes; spread via blood, semen, breast milk
  • Prevention: condom use, screening, not sharing needles
Placenta Site of exchange between mother and fetus. Oxygen, glucose, antibodies cross; fetal waste (CO₂, urea) leaves. Blood does NOT mix.
