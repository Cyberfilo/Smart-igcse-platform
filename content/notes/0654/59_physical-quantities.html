Common prefixes
  • k (kilo) = 10³ · M (mega) = 10⁶ · G (giga) = 10⁹
  • m (milli) = 10⁻³ · µ (micro) = 10⁻⁶ · n (nano) = 10⁻⁹
Measuring instruments
  • Length small: vernier callipers (0.1 mm), micrometer (0.01 mm)
  • Time short: digital stopwatch, light gates (avoid reaction time)
  • Volume: measuring cylinder, displacement for irregular solid
