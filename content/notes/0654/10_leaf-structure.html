Every part of a leaf is adapted to maximise photosynthesis.

Cross-section, top to bottom
  • Waxy cuticle — stops water loss
  • Upper epidermis — transparent, lets light through
  • Palisade mesophyll — tall cells packed with chloroplasts; main photosynthesis site
  • Spongy mesophyll — air spaces for CO₂ diffusion
  • Lower epidermis — contains guard cells
  • Stomata — gas exchange pores controlled by guard cells
  • Vascular bundle — xylem (water in) + phloem (sugars out)
Adaptations summary
