Metals arranged by reactivity. Higher metal displaces lower from its compound.

Series (most reactive at top)
K > Na > Ca > Mg > Al > Zn > Fe > Cu > Ag > Au

Reactions with water
  • K, Na, Ca — react with cold water → hydroxide + H₂
  • Mg — reacts slowly with cold water, vigorously with steam
  • Zn, Fe — react with steam → oxide + H₂
  • Cu, Ag, Au — no reaction
Reactions with dilute acid
  • Above H in series: react → salt + H₂
  • Cu, Ag, Au: no reaction (below H)
Displacement Fe + CuSO₄ → FeSO₄ + Cu (Fe more reactive). Cu + FeSO₄ → no reaction. Used in thermit process: Al + Fe₂O₃ → Fe + Al₂O₃.
H is in the series Treat H as if it were a metal between Fe and Cu. Only metals above H react with dilute acids.
