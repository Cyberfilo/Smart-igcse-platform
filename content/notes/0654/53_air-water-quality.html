Atmospheric pollutants cause specific environmental problems. Water treatment gives us safe drinking water.

Clean air composition
N₂ 78% · O₂ 21% · Ar 0.9% · CO₂ 0.04% · traces
Pollutants + consequences
COincomplete combustion · toxic, binds haemoglobin
SO₂, NOₓfrom fuels → acid rain
CO₂, CH₄greenhouse gases · trap heat → warming
Particulatessoot, PM2.5 · respiratory disease
Reducing pollution
  • Catalytic converters in cars: 2CO + 2NO → 2CO₂ + N₂
  • Flue gas desulfurisation in power stations
  • Renewable energy replaces fossil fuels
Water treatment
  1. Filtration — removes large solids
  2. Chlorination — kills microbes
  3. pH adjustment
  4. Fluoridation (in some regions)
