Animal and plant cells share a core set of organelles; plants also have a cell wall, chloroplasts, and a permanent vacuole.

Both cells have
  • Nucleus — controls activities, stores DNA
  • Cytoplasm — where reactions happen
  • Cell membrane — controls what goes in/out
  • Mitochondria — aerobic respiration
  • Ribosomes — protein synthesis
Plant-only
  • Cell wall — cellulose, gives shape and strength
  • Chloroplasts — contain chlorophyll, photosynthesis
  • Permanent vacuole — filled with cell sap, keeps cell turgid
Bacterial cells
  • No nucleus — DNA is a loop in the cytoplasm
  • Cell wall (made of peptidoglycan, not cellulose)
  • May have plasmids and flagella
Tissue → organ → system Cell → tissue (similar cells) → organ (multiple tissues with a function) → organ system (organs working together).
