A wave transfers energy without transferring matter. Key: v = fλ.

Wave vocabulary
Amplitudemax displacement from rest (m)
Wavelength λdistance between identical points (m)
Frequency fwaves per second (Hz)
Period Ttime for one wave (s); T = 1/f
Wave speedv = f × λ
Transverse vs longitudinal
Transverse
  • Oscillation ⊥ propagation
  • Light, water waves, EM waves
  • Peaks (crests) and troughs
Longitudinal
  • Oscillation ∥ propagation
  • Sound waves, seismic P waves
  • Compressions and rarefactions
Example Sound wave f = 340 Hz, v = 340 m/s. λ = v/f = 1 m.
Wave behaviours Reflection, refraction, diffraction, interference — all four occur with any wave. Ripple-tank demos show them cleanly.
