Earth is one of eight planets in the Solar System; Solar System is one of billions in the Milky Way galaxy; Milky Way is one of billions in the Universe.

Our Solar System
  • One star: the Sun
  • Eight planets (in order from Sun): Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune
  • Inner four: rocky; outer four: gas/ice giants
  • Plus dwarf planets (Pluto, Eris), asteroid belt, comets, Kuiper belt
Orbits
Planets orbit Sun in ellipses (near-circular). Gravitational attraction provides centripetal force.

Big Bang theory
  • Universe began ~13.8 billion years ago from a hot, dense state
  • Evidence: redshift (galaxies receding), cosmic microwave background
  • Expansion continues today
Star life cycle Nebula → protostar → main sequence → red giant → white dwarf (low mass) OR supernova → neutron star / black hole (high mass).
