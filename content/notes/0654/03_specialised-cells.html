Specialised cells have structure that fits their job. Surface area and shape are the universal adaptations.

Root hairHuge SA for water/mineral uptake
Red bloodBiconcave disc + no nucleus = more haemoglobin
SpermTail for motion + mitochondria
CiliatedCilia move mucus/egg cell
NerveLong axon for rapid signals
PalisadeMany chloroplasts; tall and narrow
